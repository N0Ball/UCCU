System Requirements
(Functional Requirements)

# Refnum. Description Source
FR The CubeSat shall have six modes: charging mode, Standby mode, TT&C mode, camera mode, safe mode, and phoenix mode.
FR The CubeSat shall have a testing interface to allow ground-based testing and troubleshooting.
FR Electromagnetic interference shall not inhibit CubeSat subsystems from functioning properly.
FR The CubeSat shall have a 3-axis Attitude Determination and Control System (ADCS). 1.REQ.MIS.FR.3
FR The CubeSat shall be able to communicate via UHF. 1.REQ.MIS.DC.2 1.REQ.MIS.DC.3
FR The CubeSat shall be able to transmit via S-band. 1.REQ.MIS.DC.2 1.REQ.MIS.DC.3 1.REQ.MIS.FR.1 1.REQ.MIS.FR.2
FR The CubeSat shall be able to receive and transmit from and to the ground station 1.REQ.MIS.DC.2 1.REQ.MIS.DC.3
FR The CubeSat shall maintain a power balance in all modes.
FR Electrical subsystems shall all be linked via a common electrical interface. 1.REQ.MIS.DC.1
FR The CubeSat shall maintain survival temperature in all modes for all of the components.
FR The CubeSat shall remain structurally functional. 1.REQ.MIS.DC.1
FR Mission data shall be time tagged by the onboard clock.
FR The CubeSat shall contain an instrument to perform the required commercial activity. 1.REQ.MIS.DC.1
FR Electrical subsystems shall all be able to communicate with each other

# Refnum. Description Source
